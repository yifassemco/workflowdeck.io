Enterprise-band triage

Priority fit, not just form completion

Most important decision

QuoteFlow should help Pakco find the enquiries that deserve enterprise attention.

Enterprise band Repeat volume, strong commercial signal, clear product category, and enough readiness to justify internal review.
Growth band Promising opportunity, but may need more detail, smaller follow-up, or nurturing before deeper allocation.
Needs triage Incomplete, unclear, unusual, or high-effort enquiries that need a careful decision before team time is committed.
Not a fit yet Low readiness, poor fit, missing basics, or work that may need a decline or lighter response path.
The strongest value is protecting Pakco from treating every enquiry the same.

Lead quality improvement

From inbox noise to qualified cases

What improves

Pakco can separate promising enquiries from unclear ones earlier.

Commercial fit Volume, frequency, timing, and repeat potential are captured before deep review.
Operational fit Job type, pack format, process needs, and readiness gaps are visible up front.
Information quality Missing specs, photos, QA notes, and NDA items become structured follow-up tasks.
Decision clarity The team can choose review, nurture, request more detail, escalate, or decline.
Better lead quality does not mean asking more on the first screen. It means asking the right things first, then building the case in stages.

Internal resource allocation

What the workflow helps route

The case file shows which enterprise-fit leads need Pakco time.

Routing principle

Not every enquiry needs the same internal attention.

QuoteFlow should help Pakco decide whether an enquiry is enterprise-band, needs quick sales follow-up, deeper operations review, QA input, procurement checks, or leadership escalation.

Fast path Enterprise-band fit, enough detail, known process. Sales and operations can progress quickly.
Needs detail Good potential, but specs, QA notes, packaging, or NDA items need customer follow-up.
Escalate or park Large opportunity, unusual process, unclear fit, or heavy resource requirement.
