Important finding

The app needs a clearer visibility boundary

Customer should see

Saved progress, reference number, details needed, documents needed, and how to continue the enquiry.

Pakco should see

Enterprise triage, lead quality, readiness gaps, internal flags, team allocation, approval path, and decision history.

Customer early exit should remain a draft. It should not be treated as reject. The customer can return with their reference, complete missing required information, upload files, and then submit for Pakco review.

Next focus

Discovery and triage rules

Before building the final logic, the current customer enquiry form and quoting template should be mapped into configurable triage variables.

  • Define what makes an enquiry Enterprise, Growth, or Startup / early-stage.
  • Define which fields are required before Pakco review and which are follow-up tasks.
  • Define how the dynamic form changes based on volume, frequency, product type, QA risk, documents, and sourcing needs.
  • Keep all important thresholds and rules as global variables so Pakco can tune them later.
  • Make triage, scoring, bands, and resource allocation internal-only.
